# OOTS Workflow

## Objective

Document the current Officer of the Shift (OOTS) process to identify opportunities for AI-assisted automation and decision support.

---

## Start of Shift

1. Review handover sheet from previous shift.
2. Review Outlook calendar.
3. Identify meetings scheduled during the shift.
4. Assign meeting ownership to available engineers.
5. Share meeting assignments with the team.

---

## Email Management

1. Review shared mailbox.
2. Categorise incoming emails.
3. Check historical email trails.
4. Identify whether the issue was previously handled by a team member.
5. Assign work based on:
   - Previous ownership
   - Engineer availability
   - Skill level (L1/L2/L3)
   - Current workload

### Common Challenges

- Engineers may already be overloaded.
- Tasks may be handed over repeatedly between shifts.
- Delays occur when ownership is unclear.

---

## Ticket Management

1. Review ServiceNow queue.
2. Assign tickets to engineers.
3. Monitor ticket bin every 5–10 minutes.
4. Watch for potential P1 incidents.
5. Redirect incorrectly assigned tickets to the appropriate team.

---

## P1 Incident Process

### Incident Detection

- Multiple tickets arrive simultaneously.
- Possible site outage identified.
